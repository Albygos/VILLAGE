# **App Name**: Digital Village Connect

## Core Features:

- Job Listings: Display local job opportunities, including MGNREGA projects and private sector jobs.
- Kisan Marketplace: Enable farmers to directly sell their produce to buyers and view daily market rates.
- Grievance Redressal: Allow villagers to file complaints, track their status, and provide feedback to the Panchayat.
- Announcements: Show the latest village announcements and news.
- Search: Enable users to search for jobs, market prices, or village schemes.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Forest green (#1c5f20) to evoke nature, growth, and stability.
- Background color: Light beige (#f9faf7) in light mode, and dark green (#131f14) in dark mode, to provide a clean and contrasting backdrop.
- Accent color: A slightly lighter shade of green (#34d399), analogous to the primary, to highlight interactive elements and calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for a modern, readable, and accessible design.
- Use Material Symbols Outlined for a consistent and modern look.
- Maintain a clean and spacious layout with consistent padding and margins.
- Incorporate subtle transition animations for interactive elements such as hover states.
